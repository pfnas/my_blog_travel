Sri Lanka (श्रीलंका).

Formerly called Ceylon Island, now Srilanka, a wonderful country...lovely people. A little thought for all these nice people met during this trip. Always someone to guide you, get you back on track. A little thought for Mark who welcomed me to his home as I was so lost taking my bus, to this little family with whom I spent a night in the airport parking lot and who insisted that I get married with one of their daughters.. . :) Sri Lanka, an island all in color, one rubs there the effervescence, the calms according to the places where one is. In places the jungle and the mountains, in places marvelous coastal landscapes.
