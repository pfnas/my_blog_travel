Created by the advance of glaciers in the valleys, they have given way to inlets that advance in the land at their melting. There are very steep landscapes with a fairly narrow arm of the sea. The view from a summit reveals a world in miniature, one can only be admiring in front of these incredible formations.

Habitat

Houses in Norway are built with wooden panels in blue, red or yellow colors and with slate roofs. Traditionally Norwegian houses are built cramped with small rooms to keep the heat in, and sloping roofs to help the evacuation of snow. The windows are small and there is always a fireplace or a wood oven in the center to warm the rooms in winter.
