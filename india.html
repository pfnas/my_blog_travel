Mes Pérégrinations

L'Inde (भारत).

India occupies most of the Indian subcontinent. India measures 3,214 km from north to south and 2,933 km from east to west. India is bordered to the west by the Arabian Sea (or Arabian Sea) and to the east by the Bay of Bengal, parts of the Indian Ocean. It is difficult to visit this country entirely in a few weeks because its area is so vast. By train, by bus, by boat, on foot, our journey will take us through Tamil Nandu and Kerala. A country where once out of the plane one is confronted with the temperature, the noise, the effervescence, the smells and the colors. An impression of permanent disorder. A colorful country

Informations sur l'Inde

Au départ de Barcelone, nous avons pris la compagnie Air Pégasus en direction de Chennai.

Les Français sont soumis à visa pour séjourner en Inde. Since 2015, there has been a device for issuing visas on arrival, called “e-Tourist visa”.

Jet lag : + 3h30 in summer + 4h30 in winter
